Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

At Gift Wala (operated by KISHAN CHATARARAM CHOUDHARY), we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit giftwalashop.in.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or inquire via WhatsApp, we collect information necessary to fulfill your request. This includes your name, shipping address, contact number, and the specific personalization details (such as names or photos) you wish to have engraved on our products.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to process your orders, arrange for delivery, and communicate with you regarding your purchase. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access. Any payment processing is handled through secure third-party gateways; we do not store your credit card information on our servers.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to ask for corrections if it is inaccurate. To do so, please reach out to our support team.
