Privacy Policy for The Gift Store WhatsApp Bot

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Gift Store. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our WhatsApp Business Bot.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with our WhatsApp bot, we may collect the following information:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your profile name on WhatsApp
  • The content of messages you send to the bot (including text and images)
  • Order details such as shipping address and payment preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To send you updates regarding your order status

4. Data Processing, Sharing, and Security

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is securely stored and processed in the following ways:

  • AI Processing: We use AI services (Google Gemini) to process product images and extract order details in real-time. These images are processed temporarily and are not stored permanently by the AI service.
  • Cloud Storage: Order data is stored on secure cloud databases (Supabase) hosted in data centers with industry-standard encryption.
  • Authorized Access: Data is only accessible by authorized personnel for the purpose of fulfilling your orders.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. You have the right to request the deletion of your data at any time.

To request deletion: Simply send a WhatsApp message to our bot saying "DELETE MY DATA" or email us at our official contact address. We will permanently delete your session history and order data from our active databases.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us via our WhatsApp business number.
