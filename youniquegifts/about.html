Our Story

Located in the heart of Bhayandar, Maharashtra, Younique Gifts is an exclusive boutique run by Bharat Chatararam Choudhary.


Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that true luxury lies in customization. We offer a handpicked selection of premium lifestyle products, including finely detailed wallets and uniquely engraved metallic wristwear.


Every single order that leaves our facility is treated as a masterpiece. We combine modern engraving technology with traditional craftsmanship to produce items that your loved ones will cherish forever.
